What AltekFlo Provides

Practical systems that help businesses handle sales, requests, and customer interactions more efficiently

Order Management

Ordaa converts WhatsApp conversations into structured orders. Businesses can receive requests, confirm details, track order status, and avoid losing sales in unorganized chats.

Payment Tracking

Link payments to specific orders or bookings. Businesses can clearly see what has been paid, what is pending, and what requires follow-up—reducing errors and disputes.

WhatsApp Automation

Handle transactional messages such as confirmations, updates, reminders, and support replies through WhatsApp in a controlled, policy-compliant way.

Booking & Workflow Automation

Automate service bookings, appointment requests, and repeat business processes using workflows designed around how customers already communicate on WhatsApp.

How WhatsApp Is Used

Ordaa uses the WhatsApp Business Platform strictly for transactional and operational communication, including orders, bookings, payments, and customer support, in line with Meta policies.
