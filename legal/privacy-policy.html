Privacy Policy

How AltekFlo collects, uses, and protects information

Effective Date: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how The AltekFlo Enterprise (“AltekFlo”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use the AltekFlo website and platform (https://altekflo.xyz).

1. Company Information

AltekFlo is a commerce and automation software platform operated by The AltekFlo Enterprise, a legally registered business in Nigeria.

Official contact email: support@altekflo.xyz

2. Information We Collect

We collect only information necessary to provide our services. This may include:

  • Business or store name
  • Contact email address
  • WhatsApp phone number
  • Order, payment, and transaction details
  • Messages exchanged through the platform

3. How We Use Information

Information collected is used strictly to:

  • Operate and maintain the AltekFlo platform
  • Process orders, payments, and fulfillment workflows
  • Send transactional WhatsApp notifications
  • Provide customer and technical support
  • Improve platform reliability and security

4. WhatsApp Messaging Policy

AltekFlo integrates with the WhatsApp Business Platform to enable transactional messaging only.

Messages sent through the platform may include:

  • Order confirmations
  • Payment status updates
  • Pickup or delivery notifications
  • Customer support responses

AltekFlo does not send unsolicited promotional, marketing, or bulk messaging. All WhatsApp usage is designed to comply with Meta’s WhatsApp Business Policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Information may be shared only with trusted third-party service providers (such as messaging or payment processors) strictly for the purpose of operating the platform and only where required.

6. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure.

7. Data Retention

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide our services, meet operational requirements, or comply with applicable legal obligations.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at support@altekflo.xyz.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
